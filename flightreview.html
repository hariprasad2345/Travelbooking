Indigo Airlines

IndiGo is a popular low-cost airline based in India. Here are some key details about IndiGo:

Overview

•Founded: 2006
• Headquarters: Gurugram, Haryana, India
• Hubs: Indira Gandhi International Airport (Delhi), Chhatrapati Shivaji Maharaj International Airport (Mumbai),
• Fleet Size: One of the largest in India, primarily consisting of Airbus A320 family aircraft, including A320neo,

Destinations

• Domestic: IndiGo serves numerous cities across India, connecting major urban centers and regional locations.
• International: The airline also operates flights to several international destinations, including in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Services

• In-Flight Services: As a low-cost carrier, IndiGo offers a range of services including paid meals, beverages,
and entertainment. The airline emphasizes punctuality and efficiency.

• Loyalty Program: IndiGo has a loyalty program called "6E Rewards," which offers benefits and rewards to frequent flyers.
