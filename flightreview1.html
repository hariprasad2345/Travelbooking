qatar airlines

Qatar Airways is one of the world's leading airlines, known for its premium service and extensive global network. Here's an overview

Overview

Founded: 1993
Headquarters: Doha,
Hub: Hamad International Airport (DOH)
Ownership: State-owned by the Qatari government

Destinations

• Domestic: IndiGo serves numerous cities across India, connecting major urban centers and regional locations.
• International: The airline also operates flights to several international destinations, including in Asia, the Middle East, and Africa.

Services

• First Class: Features include lie-flat seats, fine dining, and premium amenities.

• Business Class: Renowned for its Qsuite, offering private cabins with lie-flat seats, fine dining, and high-quality service.
